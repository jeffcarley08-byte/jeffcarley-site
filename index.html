Signature Topics

Three Talks I’m Known For

Starting points, not a fixed menu. Every talk is tailored to your audience, sized to your format, and built around your event theme.

🌿

ROOTS — The Power of Community

The biosphere tree grew fast but collapsed under real weight. The redwood grows alongside other trees, their roots intermingled. This talk shows leaders how to build the root system that holds everything else up.

Audience Leaves With:
A named understanding of why isolation kills leadership
The CCC Framework: Communication, Community, Consistency
One specific action to build trust with their team this week
⛰️

Chasing Summits

The climb never really ends — and that’s the point. This talk reframes leadership as a continuous ascent and gives your audience a new way to think about growing toward the next peak, not just resting on the last one.

Audience Leaves With:
A four-terrain model for identifying where they are right now
The specific tools for their current terrain — not generic advice
One next move, named and committed to before they leave the room
🛠️

The Craft of Leadership

Leadership isn’t a personality trait. It’s a craft — learnable, improvable, and worth treating with the same discipline as any other skilled trade. Built on 4,500 hours of teaching it.

Audience Leaves With:
A new mental model: leader as craftsman, not personality type
The three things that separate good leaders from great ones
A concrete development practice they can start Monday
